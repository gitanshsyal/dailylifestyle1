Person who won World Cup for its nation When he was Suffering from Cancer-Yuvraj Singh
We know how critical is to fight the battle of any disease that comes with a twitch of tug of war between life and death.Well,for common human beings,the fear of cancer is Something that kills them before even it attacks them.
While talking about Cancer,the lion of Punjab who given us enormous moments in cricket to cheer for.From cricket to cancer,Yuvraj has made every single obstacle an opportunity while celebrating the journey-be it through the toughest of hardships and emerge as a fighter to give the entire world an inspiration Comeback.
Yuvraj Singh's Cancer story is a living treatment of how far a human soul can go and how much power each of us have ourselves if we have the zeal to fight back to life for the passion we have.Yuvi is the man who revoltionized the fielding standards of Indians and also the hero of 2007 and 2011 World cups.Morever,he is man who refused to give up and played the 2011 CWC though he was struggling to manage his breath,frequent vomiting,lung pain and nausea because he chose to give his best than losing it to his adverse health situations.He was unable to sleep that nights,he had to eat painkillers and sleeping pills for Sleep.He Said-" He know he could die if he didn't give up cricket but he said he would love to die on the field of cricket".His Cricket journey started with playing for Punjab under 19 and he shone brighter than a star when he won man of the match in 2000 under 19 world cup and since then there is no looking back.
This "Punjab da Puttar" has given us wonderful moments from 2002 in Lords to ICC World T20 in 2007 to ICC World Cup in 2011.Hardly did he know that this winning glory was waiting to overshadow him by the deadly disease he has been carrying in his lung-a huge tumor was pressing against Yuvi's left lung and artery.Admidst the breathelessness and bouts of nausea,he was constantly in a dilemma whether he would be to come back to life as a winner defeating cancer and live life even bigger or was that going to be end of it.
While talking about his difficult and wobble jorney.Yuvi says,
"I was inspired By Lance Armstrong when i was going through Cancer.It's tough when you go through a period like this in your life when you don't know where your life is going to go."
He said only thing that kept him going was his mental strength that he gained being a sportsman for so many years.As the treatment was long and mentally draining phase of his life,he said it was important to be mentally srong since stronger one is mental,the stronger the body starts believing it is.He went stating he had to work harder on his body to prepare for a healthy comeback to follow his dreams and achieve more in cricket.
